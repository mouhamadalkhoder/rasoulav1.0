SHIP MANAGEMENT:

Rassoula Maritime is a global third-party ship manager that offers a full suite of integrated marine services thanks to a combination of traditional maritime skills and cutting-edge technology.

TRADING SERVICES

Rassoula Maritime's shipbroking services are unrivaled in terms of the number and caliber of brokers , the breadth of market coverage, geographical spread, and depth of intelligence resources.We are involved in every significant sector of shipping, covering virtually every type of ship and cargo, and we provide 24/7 global coverage from offices on five continents.

DRY CARGO

Rassoula Maritime has the most global presence in dry cargo shipbroking of any company, with offices in 13 countries. They employ highly experienced brokers, operations personnel, and market analysts who cover all of the major dry bulk routes and vessel sizes. Freight coverage ranges from short-term spot and affreightment contracts to long-term time charters with re-letting period deals and options. Freight analysis, project consulting, business strategy, and post-fixture work are examples of value-added in-house services. The dry bulk market includes iron ore, coal, grains, steel, cement, timber, gypsum, forest products, agricultural products, nonferrous minerals, and metals.

SALES AND PURCHASES

having one of the worlds largest and most experienced teams of S & P brokers, we cover the new building, secondhand, and demolition markets of ocean-going tonnage over 10,000dwt up to Capesize and VLCCs from offices in London, Dubai, Hong Kong, Piraeus, Singapore, and Shanghai. There are teams dedicated to the dry (bulkers) and wet (tankers) markets, containers, gas, ro-ro vessels, and offshore. Clients include the world's leading independent and publicly traded ship-owning companies, as well as all of Europe's and the Far East's major shipyards. We have direct access to up-to-date shipping intelligence services, which are now available to our clients. The S & P division specializes in project business, particularly complex sale and leaseback transactions, and has a track record of closing deals involving entire fleets as well as blocks of vessels.

DEMOLITION VESSEL

We are currently offering new services such as demolition vessels. If you have a vessel for scrap, we can sell it now for demolition in Aliaga-Turkey. Please do not hesitate to contact us at any time. We can guarantee you the best price and performance in the Aliaga region.
